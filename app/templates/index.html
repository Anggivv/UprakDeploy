ICA

Iris ClAssifier


This application is made to determine the species of the iris flower based on the measurement of length and width of sepals and petals flowers. "Iris Setosa", "Iris Versicolor", and "Iris Virginica" are the three species of iris that can be predicted through this application. The user only needs to provide 4 measurement values to be able to find out the species of the iris. The four measurement values are the length of petals, width of petals, length of sepals, and the width of the sepals.
